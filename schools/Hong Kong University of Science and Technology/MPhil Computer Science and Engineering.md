### SOP
**Source:** https://blog.sopwriteronline.com/sop-for-computer-science

The webpage is a guide for writing a Statement of Purpose (SOP) for Computer Science at UG, MS, and PhD levels. It emphasizes that the SOP must explain the applicant's academic background, interest in technology, relevant projects or work experience, reasons for choosing a specific degree and university, and clear career goals. Distinctively, the guide stresses tailoring the SOP to the degree level—focusing on potential for undergraduates, technical depth for MS, and research direction for PhD—and advises connecting work experience to academic motivation.

Tips: Tailor your SOP's technical depth and focus to the degree level (e.g., potential for UG, specialization for MS, research for PhD).; If you have work experience, highlight challenges, skills gained, and knowledge gaps that led you back to academia, not just job duties.; Ensure a clear, logical structure that connects your academic progression, goals, and university choice.
