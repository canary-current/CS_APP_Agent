## Admission Statistics

### Source: https://www.cs.hku.hk/programmes/research-based/mphil-phd-admission

The School of Computing & Data Science at HKU offers PhD and MPhil programs with no specific GPA or GRE thresholds published. Admission is competitive, with early recruitment rounds inviting applicants from premier universities worldwide. The main round closes December 1, 2026, and early application is advised. A research proposal is required, and English proficiency tests (TOEFL iBT 85+) are needed for non-English medium institutions.

Tips: Apply early in the early recruitment rounds (April or August 2026) to improve your chances and be considered for interviews.; Prepare a strong research proposal that clearly describes your proposed approach and fit with faculty research areas.; Check individual professor recruitment messages and tailor your application to align with their expectations.

HKU's MPhil/PhD programmes in Computing and Data Science do not publish specific admission statistics such as typical GPA ranges, GRE scores, or acceptance rates.
